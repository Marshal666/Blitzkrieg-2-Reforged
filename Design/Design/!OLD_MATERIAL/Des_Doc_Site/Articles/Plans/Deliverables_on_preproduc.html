Deliverables_on_preproduction

Deliverables:

1.      Design vision document “b2-concept.rtf” in Word, 2-3 pages, includes:

1.1   Marketing pitching 

1.2   Market positioning (see 1.1)

1.3   Key Selling Points (see 1.1)

1.4   Comparison with Blitzkrieg-1 and major competitors

2.      Design document in HTML (CityDesk) format in Russian and Word format in English:

2.1   Detailed feature list (expanded Key Selling Points list); some can be marked as optional

2.2   Detailed analysis of current and future announced competitors feature by feature (see 1.4 and competitor's selling points)

2.3   Analysis of Blitzkrieg-1 strong and weak sides and ways to improve

2.4   Description of intermission gameplay

2.5   Changes to mission gameplay in comparison with Blitzkrieg-1

2.6   Analyses of game balance based on Blitzkrieg-1 (also Tracks and Reinforcements)

2.7   Description of multiplayer gameplay, modes, number of maps, ways to organize player’s community

2.8   Scenario, including descriptions of all major characters, list of chapters, number of missions in chapters (missions descriptions are not required), description of seasons and settings of each chapter

2.9   List of all units in game (error within +/-5%)

2.10     List of all structured, buildings and objects in the game (error within +/-5%)

2.11     Description of all FMV sequences, their goals, length, mood, ideas for scenario (scenario itself is presented by video department)

2.12     Description of in-mission interface, hotkeys

2.13     Logical description of major interface screens, their purposes and necessary actions on each of them (optional)

2.14     Description of soundtrack and voice over in terms of what sound is required, where, how much, what mood should it set

2.15     Description of desired level and way to support historical correctness

2.16     System requirements and expected performance

2.17     Description of ELK and Editor

2.18     Description of tutorials and learning curve

2.19     Description of technology for creating movies based on in-game engine

3.      Artproofs. Results of art experiments in the form of HTML document with  screenshots, movies and texts explaining what decisions were made and why. Artproofs should include:

·        Most buildings sketches

·        2+ different units as visible in the game and their destruction

·        2 different buildings and their phases of destruction

·        2+ different trees and animation of destruction

·        2+ different soldiers, animated

·        1 explosion with smoke effects, animated

·        terrain and ocean textures

·        camera as positioned in the game showing minimum, normal, and maximum zoom

4.      Technology (performance) demo, including:

4.1        Full 3D terrain with steep hillsides, mountains, etc.

4.2        Ocean costal line with dynamic waves

4.3        The following number of objects should be displayed simultaneously on target configuration with minimum 30 fps:

·  50 animated, rotating infantries (down from 200 in Blitzkrieg)

·  25 rotating tanks

·  10 buildings

·  25 trees

·  10 explosions cycling

5.      List of milestones for publisher b2-milestones.rtf (this document)

6.      Work plan “b2-schedule.mpp” (milestones, people, other resources used, budget) for the whole project

7.      People/month time load table for the whole project

8.      Risks assessment “b2-risks.rtf” listing top 10 risks, probability of them happening, consequences, and ways to minimize them

9.      “b2-reuse.xls” middleware / code reuse solutions internal document (Excel)

10.   “b2-middleware.rtf” middleware list (MS Word, 1 page, English)

All documents, art proofs and tech demo should be packaged onto one CD and accompanied by some materials from the above list printed in a binder.
